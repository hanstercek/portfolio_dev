Born and raised in Beverly Hills, California, Hans Tercek currently attends Tufts University in Medford, MA, where he is pursuing a B.S. Mechanical Engineering with minor in Computer Science.
At Tufts, Hans is actively involved as a member of the Tufts Hybrid Race Team, captains Tufts Club Water Polo, serves as Director of Diversity & Inclusion for Delta Tau Delta - Beta Mu, and is a radio host for WMFO.
Previously, Hans attended Beverly Hills High School, where he founded a radio station, played water polo and lacrosse, and captained the school's FRC build team.
Hans is interested in robotics, the Internet of Things, product development, and explores music in his free time.
